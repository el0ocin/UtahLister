# Brand And Positioning

This is the canonical brand direction for UtahLister.

## Brand Core

- Brand name: `UtahLister`
- Brand type: Utah marketplace listing studio
- Slogan: `Clearer listings. Better buyers. Less seller work.`
- Positioning statement: `UtahLister helps Utah sellers turn photos and item details into stronger Facebook Marketplace and KSL listings without handing over their account or payout.`

## The Job The Brand Must Do

The brand needs to make strangers feel three things quickly:

- this is real
- this is local
- this will make the selling process easier without getting weird

That means UtahLister should sound like a calm operator, not a hypey growth brand.

## What UtahLister Is

UtahLister is:

- trust-first
- local
- practical
- proof-backed
- service-led

UtahLister is not:

- an AI agency
- a reseller coach
- a broker
- a shipping business
- a generic assistant-for-hire

## Messaging Pillars

### 1. Relief

UtahLister removes the hardest parts of selling:

- pricing
- writing
- photo order
- first-message handling

### 2. Trust

UtahLister keeps the seller relationship clean:

- seller keeps the account
- seller keeps the payout
- seller keeps control of the item

### 3. Proof

UtahLister should talk in specifics:

- item type
- lane change
- clicks
- conversations
- time-to-sale

### 4. Local credibility

UtahLister is built for Utah pickup selling, which means KSL and Facebook Marketplace matter more than generic ecommerce advice.

## Approved One-Liners

### Short version

`UtahLister is a Utah marketplace listing studio for sellers who want stronger listings with less work on their side.`

### Direct service line

`Send the photos and details you already have. Get back a stronger Marketplace and KSL listing package.`

### Trust line

`You keep the account and payout. UtahLister builds the listing package.`

## Voice Rules

Write like:

- a calm local operator
- a practical guide
- a brand that values trust over hype

Do not write like:

- a vague AI product
- a manipulative ad funnel
- a reseller coach selling status

## Proof Policy

- Customer proof is the long-term goal and should lead the brand.
- Founder proof is allowed only when clearly labeled as an example case study.
- Never let founder proof look like customer proof.
- Never use vague claims when a labeled specific example would do a better job.

## Visual Direction

Use:

- warm neutral backgrounds
- dark charcoal text
- one canyon-like warm accent
- strong typography with premium restraint

Avoid:

- loud novelty for its own sake
- dark dashboard aesthetics
- generic SaaS UI signaling
- cluttered collage-style layouts
