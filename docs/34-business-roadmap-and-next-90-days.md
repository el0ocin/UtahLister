# Business Roadmap And Next 90 Days

This is the business execution roadmap for UtahLister.

Use [15-utahlister-product-roadmap.md](15-utahlister-product-roadmap.md) for the longer-term productization and software direction. Use this document for the practical business sequence.

## Current Phase

UtahLister is in `proof-and-repeatability` mode.

That means the focus is:

- making the one-item service work cleanly
- getting repeatable customer outcomes
- tightening trust, delivery, and positioning
- avoiding unnecessary software complexity before proof exists

## Phase 1: Foundation Defined

Status: mostly complete

What is already in place:

- target customer definition
- offer and pricing structure
- operating workflow
- posting model
- outreach system
- website direction
- research library
- brand and positioning guidance

## Phase 2: Proof And Repeatability

Status: active

Primary goal:

- prove that UtahLister can repeatedly turn one clean intake into one successful listing outcome with low founder chaos

What matters in this phase:

- clean one-item intake
- consistent pricing lane selection
- strong listing package delivery
- real proof capture
- real testimonials
- stable follow-up rhythm

## Phase 3: Controlled Launch

Status: not complete

Primary goal:

- launch publicly with a service that is small, clean, and trustworthy

Requirements before this phase:

- working public intake
- clean seller-owned posting model
- enough proof to support trust claims
- stable delivery workflow
- founder confidence in turnaround and follow-up

## Phase 4: Systems Hardening

Status: future

Primary goal:

- make the business more efficient without increasing trust or policy risk

Examples:

- internal dashboarding
- more structured proof tracking
- more reliable intake handling
- safer and more repeatable private tooling

## Next 90 Days

### Priority 1

Make the one-item service operationally clean.

That means:

- finalize the public-safe repo and business handbook
- keep the offer simple
- keep the seller-owned-account model explicit
- tighten intake and delivery checklists

### Priority 2

Run enough real jobs to generate proof.

That means:

- complete real customer or partner-referred items
- document outcomes
- collect proof and testimonials
- learn where the workflow breaks

### Priority 3

Tighten the public trust layer.

That means:

- clarify the website promise
- make the intake path obvious
- use proof carefully and honestly
- keep the brand grounded and local

### Priority 4

Stabilize the private operating layer.

That means:

- keep private code and tooling out of the public repo
- document the private systems at a business-summary level only
- make sure tooling supports the service instead of driving it

## What Not To Prioritize Yet

- full autoposting
- broad automation for its own sake
- multi-platform sprawl
- high-complexity dashboards
- public claims that are not yet backed by proof

## Success Definition

UtahLister is ready for the next phase when:

- the founder can run jobs without improvising the workflow every time
- proof and testimonials exist
- the public-facing story matches the actual service
- the private tooling supports delivery without becoming the product itself
