# UtahLister Company Knowledge Base

## Purpose

This repo is the company-owned, public-safe source of truth for UtahLister strategy, offer design, workflow decisions, growth thinking, and business-state summaries.

It is meant to answer:

- what the business is
- who it serves
- how the service works
- how the business is supposed to grow
- what has already been built
- what still needs to happen before launch

## What This Knowledge Base Is For

Use this repo to preserve:

- durable business decisions
- operating rules that are safe to share publicly
- research-backed summaries
- approved messaging and outreach guidance
- sanitized templates
- roadmap and progress snapshots

## What This Knowledge Base Is Not For

Do not use this repo for:

- private code or application architecture
- customer data or screenshots
- credentials or access details
- private workspace configuration
- raw security findings with implementation detail
- temporary operating clutter

## Knowledge Rules

1. The repo should explain the business clearly before it tries to explain tooling.
2. Public-safe summaries belong here; sensitive implementation details do not.
3. Research should be stored as summaries, synthesis, and source maps rather than raw scraped material.
4. Proof should be represented through case studies and results summaries, not raw customer screenshots.
5. Private systems can be described at a business level, but their code and exact internals stay outside this repo.

## Maintenance Standard

After any meaningful business change:

1. update the relevant doc
2. update the current-state or roadmap summary if the change is material
3. keep the repo consistent with the seller-owned-account, trust-first business model
4. remove any accidental references to private code, customer data, or sensitive setup details
