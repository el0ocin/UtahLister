# UtahLister Offer And Pricing

## Core positioning

UtahLister is not selling software access.

It is selling a stronger local-sale listing package with less seller work and clearer pricing discipline.

Core promise:

`UtahLister helps Utah sellers build or rescue Marketplace and KSL listings while the seller keeps the account, item, meetup, and payout.`

## Service paths

UtahLister now has two public service paths:

### `New Listing Build`

Best fit when:

- the seller has not posted yet
- the seller already has photos and basic facts
- the seller wants a clean listing package from scratch

### `Listing Rescue`

Best fit when:

- the listing is already live
- it is stale, underpriced, or weakly presented
- the seller needs a relist or rewrite plan more than a blank-page build

## Who this is for

- overwhelmed local sellers
- people moving, downsizing, or clearing clutter
- sellers with decent items but weak listing execution
- Utah sellers who want local pickup and simple handoff
- people who want help without handing over the account or payout

## Who this is not for

- full-time flippers who need bulk automation first
- national shipping businesses
- clients who want guaranteed sale prices or sale windows
- policy-sensitive or restricted categories
- extremely low-value items with no margin to rescue

## New Listing Build beta pricing

Current working one-item pricing:

- under `$50` expected sale value: `$5`
- `$50-$149`: `$15`
- `$150-$299`: `$29`
- `$300-$599`: `$49`
- `$600+`: custom beta quote

## Listing Rescue working pricing

Current recommended rescue structure:

- `Rescue Audit`: `$19`
- `Standard One-Item Rescue`: `$29`
- `Bulky / Complex Rescue`: `$49`
- `Premium / Custom Rescue`: `$79+`
- `Simple multi-item packs`: `3 for $79`, `5 for $129`

Public rule:

- do not lead with a commission or success fee
- keep rescue as a clear flat-fee service
- use value and complexity gates instead of vague quoting

## Pricing language to use

Lead with the seller's goal and let UtahLister supply the numbers.

Use:

- `Quick Sale Price`
- `Max Profit Price`
- `Private Floor`
- `Selected Lane`

For rescue work, also use:

- `Current Live Price`
- `Recommended Rescue Price`
- `Relist Or Edit Recommendation`

## What the client gets per item

- selected lane: `quick sale` or `max profit`
- recommended live posted price
- Facebook title and description
- KSL title and description
- cover-photo and gallery-order recommendation
- buyer reply prompts
- guided posting or relisting support when needed
- 48-hour review note

For rescue jobs, the package can also include:

- rescue diagnosis
- missing-detail prompts
- category or framing correction
- relist recommendation

## What is not included in v1

- long market reports
- custom dashboards for clients
- photo retouching as a public promise
- shipping workflows
- eBay, Mercari, and Poshmark by default
- guaranteed sale windows
- direct buyer handling by default

## Delivery promise

`UtahLister turns the item photos, listing URL, and facts you already have into a stronger listing package and a faster path to posting or relisting.`

## Sales guardrails

- promise speed and clarity, not guaranteed outcomes
- promise honest, local positioning, not miracle pricing
- keep buyer reply prompts inside the core offer
- do not sell the internal stack or app as the product
- raise pricing only after more proof and repeatable completion data exist
