# UtahLister Offer

## Core positioning
You are not selling "AI."

You are selling relief, speed, and better sell-through:

`Send UtahLister the photos and details you already have, and get back a stronger Marketplace + KSL listing with less work on your side.`

## Who this is for
- overwhelmed local sellers
- people moving, downsizing, or clearing clutter
- people who already have photos but keep delaying the actual post
- normal sellers who do not want a big system
- Utah sellers who want local pickup and simple handoff

## Who this is not for
- full-time flippers who need bulk automation first
- national shipping businesses
- clients who want heavy photo editing or design work
- clients who expect guaranteed sale prices

## Beta offer
- Public brand: `UtahLister`
- Scope: one item at a time
- Platforms included: Facebook Marketplace + KSL
- Turnaround: within 48 hours after clean intake
- Cross-post add-on: only when it clearly helps

## Beta pricing bands
- under `$50` expected sale value: `$5`
- `$50-$149`: `$15`
- `$150-$299`: `$29`
- `$300-$599`: `$49`
- `$600+`: custom beta quote

## Pricing language to use
Lead with the seller's goal and let UtahLister supply the numbers.

Use:
- `Quick Sale Price`: what UtahLister would post at when speed matters and the goal is likely movement in `3-7 days`
- `Max Profit Price`: what UtahLister would post at when the seller can wait and the goal is a stronger number over `14-21 days`
- `Private Floor`: optional seller-only backup number if they already know their hard no-go line
- `Selected Lane`: the lane UtahLister recommends for this item based on the seller's goal and the local read

## What the client gets per item
- a selected lane: `quick sale` or `max profit`
- `Quick Sale Price`
- `Max Profit Price`
- the recommended live posted price
- Facebook title and short description
- KSL title and short description
- best cover photo and image order
- buyer reply script pack
- guided posting support when needed
- live listing links once the item is posted
- a 48-hour review note

## Buyer reply packaging rule
- keep `buyer reply scripts` inside the core offer
- do not sell scripts by themselves as a public add-on yet
- if a future upsell is justified, name it `Buyer Handling Assist`
- `Buyer Handling Assist` means custom objection handling, lowball handling, negotiation prompts, and optional live reply help
- do not expose that upsell publicly until outside research and internal demand data both support it

## What is not included in v1
- long market reports
- custom dashboards for clients
- photo retouching
- shipping workflows
- eBay, Mercari, and Poshmark by default
- guaranteed sale windows

## Delivery promise
`UtahLister turns the item photos and details you already have into stronger Marketplace and KSL listings, clearer pricing, sharper buyer replies, and a faster path to posting.`

## How to talk about the service
Short version:

`I help Utah sellers turn photo folders into live marketplace listings fast.`

Longer version:

`If you have stuff to sell but keep putting it off, UtahLister turns your item photos and notes into ready-to-post Marketplace and KSL listings and supports the posting process without making you build it from scratch yourself.`

## Pricing notes
- keep beta cheap on purpose
- prioritize proof, testimonials, and real completion data over margin
- do not jump back to a 3-item bundle until the one-item offer converts smoothly
- raise pricing only after the service has real proof and tighter average completion times

## Upgrade path after beta
- `Starter Post`: one item, fixed fee
- `Small Batch`: 3 to 5 items once there is proof that the workflow stays clean
- `Repricing Rescue`: fix stale listings that are underperforming
- `Buyer Handling Assist`: optional higher-touch reply and negotiation support once demand is proven

## Sales guardrails
- promise speed and clarity, not guaranteed outcomes
- promise honest, local positioning, not miracle pricing
- sell the finished live listing, not the internal stack
- track time-to-first-response, inquiry-to-pickup rate, and lowball-to-full-price recovery before changing reply packaging
