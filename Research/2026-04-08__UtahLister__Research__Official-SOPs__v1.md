## 1. Master Workflow Summary

UtahLister’s operating model is clear: the customer owns the posting account, keeps the item, meets the buyer, and keeps the payout; UtahLister creates the listing package, guides posting if needed, logs proof, and runs a 48-hour review. The research also tightened the rough workflow by adding explicit intake validation, eligibility screening, payment-before-production, QC gates, live-listing verification, and a formal evidence packet at closeout.   

For solo-operator usability, this SOP set uses **10 official SOPs** by combining the architecture’s first two internal states into one front-end SOP. That keeps the operating system lean while staying faithful to the approved workflow logic. This is a **recommended assumption for documentation structure**, not a change to the underlying workflow rules.  

**Master workflow:**
Intake, Validation & Eligibility → Asset Review & Clarifications → Service Lane & Quote Confirmation → Payment Confirmation & Scheduling → Listing Package Drafting → QC & Policy Compliance → Delivery & Posting Handoff → Live Posting Verification → URL Logging & 48-Hour Review → Proof Capture & Closeout

---

## 2. Official Stage List

1. **Intake, Validation & Eligibility** — create the job, confirm basic readiness, and reject risky or unfit jobs early.
2. **Asset Review & Clarifications** — review photos/details deeply enough to write without guessing.
3. **Service Lane & Quote Confirmation** — define scope, price, turnaround, and revision boundaries.
4. **Payment Confirmation & Scheduling** — collect payment and place the job into production.
5. **Listing Package Drafting** — build the actual customer deliverable.
6. **QC & Policy Compliance** — verify accuracy, clarity, completeness, and platform fit.
7. **Delivery & Posting Handoff** — send the copy/paste-ready package and posting instructions.
8. **Live Posting Verification** — confirm the customer posted correctly and the listing is live.
9. **URL Logging & 48-Hour Review** — log URLs, capture metrics, and recommend one decisive optimization if needed.
10. **Proof Capture & Closeout** — store the evidence packet and mark the job complete.

## 4. SOP 1

* **SOP Name:** Intake, Validation & Eligibility
* **Stage Purpose:** Create a clean job record, confirm the submission is workable, and stop bad-fit jobs before they waste time.
* **Trigger:** New intake arrives through form, email, DM, or other approved intake channel.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Customer contact info, item name/basic identity, condition notes, asking price or goal, location/pickup area, photos, any included-item notes.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Create a job record and assign a Job ID.
  2. Send intake confirmation.
  3. Check required fields for completeness.
  4. Check whether the item is allowed and fits UtahLister’s boundaries.
  5. Mark the job as one of three statuses: Ready for Asset Review, Needs Info, or Rejected.
  6. If rejected, send a short explanation and close the job.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Can UtahLister identify the item well enough to proceed?
  * Is there enough truth-first information to avoid guessing?
  * Does the item pass policy/risk screening?
  * Has the customer been reminded that UtahLister does not take passwords or post from customer accounts?
* **Outputs:** Job record, intake status, confirmation message, clarification request or rejection message.
* **Done Criteria:** Job is either moved to Asset Review & Clarifications, paused as Needs Info, or closed as Rejected.
* **Next Stage:** Asset Review & Clarifications
* **Common Exceptions:** Incomplete intake, prohibited/restricted item, unclear item identity, no usable photo, wrong-fit request.
* **Saved Records:** Job ID, intake timestamp, raw intake data, initial photos/files, eligibility decision, reason code if rejected.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:**

  * Confirmation: “Got it — we received your item and are reviewing it for fit and completeness.”
  * Needs Info: “We need a few missing details before we can proceed.”
  * Rejected: “We can’t take this item/job as submitted. Here’s the reason.”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rules: seller-owned posting accounts, no password collection, pause on risky/restricted items. Recommended assumption: this SOP combines job creation and eligibility screening into one front-end stage for solo use.

## 5. SOP 2

* **SOP Name:** Asset Review & Clarifications
* **Stage Purpose:** Verify the photos and details are good enough to produce an accurate listing package.
* **Trigger:** SOP 1 finishes with a workable, non-rejected job.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Accepted intake, uploaded photos, condition notes, brand/model info if known, seller goal, included-item notes.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Review all submitted photos for clarity, coverage, and truthfulness.
  2. Check whether the item’s identity, condition, flaws, and included parts can be described accurately.
  3. Note any missing details or missing shots.
  4. Send one consolidated clarification request rather than scattered follow-ups.
  5. Re-check new inputs when received.
  6. Move the job forward once the clarification list is resolved.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Are photos clear enough to support a listing?
  * Are visible flaws documented?
  * Is there enough coverage to choose a cover photo and image order?
  * Can UtahLister write without inventing facts?
* **Outputs:** Asset review notes, clarification list, updated status, resolved asset set.
* **Done Criteria:** Photos and details meet minimum quality and coverage, or the job is paused/closed under exception rules.
* **Next Stage:** Service Lane & Quote Confirmation
* **Common Exceptions:** Insufficient photos, blurry images, missing defect shots, conflicting condition info, customer sends data piecemeal.
* **Saved Records:** Asset review notes, clarification messages, additional files received, final “ready for quote” timestamp.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “We reviewed your item. To do this right, we still need [specific details/photos].”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: photo quality and information completeness are separate checks. Recommended assumption: clarification requests should be bundled into one message whenever possible.

## 6. SOP 3

* **SOP Name:** Service Lane & Quote Confirmation
* **Stage Purpose:** Define what the customer is buying, how the item will be positioned, and what boundaries apply before work begins.
* **Trigger:** Asset review confirms the job is workable.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Clean intake, reviewed assets, job complexity assessment, seller goal, any scope-impacting notes.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Separate **Service Lane** from **Listing Strategy**.
  2. Determine scope: core package only, or package plus approved add-ons.
  3. Confirm turnaround promise and included revision policy.
  4. Send quote or pricing confirmation.
  5. Wait for explicit customer acceptance before requesting payment.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Is this a standard lane or a custom quote?
  * Are add-ons clearly separated from core scope?
  * Is the listing strategy clear: quick sale, max profit, or not sure?
  * Are boundaries explicit enough to prevent scope creep?
* **Outputs:** Confirmed quote, scope summary, turnaround promise, revision boundary, lane decision.
* **Done Criteria:** Customer has a written quote/scope and UtahLister has a clear go/no-go scope record.
* **Next Stage:** Payment Confirmation & Scheduling
* **Common Exceptions:** Outside pricing lane, unusual research burden, bundle ambiguity, customer asks for extras not included.
* **Saved Records:** Service lane, listing strategy, quote amount, included deliverables, excluded deliverables, turnaround, revision rules.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “Here’s what’s included, what’s not included, your turnaround, and your total.”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: service tier and item pricing strategy must not be mixed. **DECISION NEEDED:** final lane names, thresholds, and pricing are not yet fully locked in the research set.

## 7. SOP 4

* **SOP Name:** Payment Confirmation & Scheduling
* **Stage Purpose:** Secure payment before production and place the job into the production queue.
* **Trigger:** Customer accepts the quote and scope.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Accepted quote, customer contact, payment link/invoice, written scope.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Send invoice or payment link.
  2. Confirm payment cleared.
  3. Save the invoice/receipt and associated scope.
  4. Assign production due date.
  5. Move the job to Paid / Queued.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Has payment fully cleared?
  * Are invoice, receipt, and scope stored together?
  * Has the production clock started only after complete intake + payment?
* **Outputs:** Paid status, scheduled due date, invoice/receipt record.
* **Done Criteria:** Payment is confirmed and the job is scheduled for production.
* **Next Stage:** Listing Package Drafting
* **Common Exceptions:** Overdue invoice, failed payment, payment dispute, partial payment.
* **Saved Records:** Invoice ID, payment timestamp, amount paid, payment status, due date, proof-of-payment reference.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “Payment received. Your item is now queued, and your package is due by [date/time or turnaround window].”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: payment happens before production. Recommended assumption: incomplete intake pauses the turnaround clock.

## 8. SOP 5

* **SOP Name:** Listing Package Drafting
* **Stage Purpose:** Create the actual customer deliverable: pricing plan, listing copy, photo order, category/platform guidance, and buyer replies.
* **Trigger:** Payment is confirmed and the job is scheduled.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Paid job, reviewed assets, seller goal, scope record, location/pickup info, any clarified details.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Review the item and seller goal.
  2. Pull local comps and note pricing references.
  3. Set Quick Sale Price, Max Profit Price, and recommended posted price.
  4. Choose the strongest cover photo and image order.
  5. Draft title options.
  6. Draft the description with condition, inclusions, flaws, and terms.
  7. Select category/platform guidance.
  8. Draft buyer reply lines.
  9. Assemble the Listing Package in copy/paste-ready format.
  10. Self-review before formal QC.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Does the package include all promised parts?
  * Are pricing decisions explained enough to defend internally?
  * Are copy blocks mobile-friendly?
  * Does the draft match the customer’s stated goal?
* **Outputs:** Draft Listing Package.
* **Done Criteria:** Complete draft package exists and is ready for formal QC.
* **Next Stage:** QC & Policy Compliance
* **Common Exceptions:** Hard-to-price item, missing late-arriving info, scope creep, inconsistent seller facts.
* **Saved Records:** Draft version, pricing rationale summary, cover photo choice, category/platform notes, buyer reply draft.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** Usually none during drafting unless a new blocker appears.
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: UtahLister’s core product is a posting-ready Listing Package containing pricing, copy, image order, and reply prompts.

## 9. SOP 6

* **SOP Name:** QC & Policy Compliance
* **Stage Purpose:** Verify that the draft is complete, accurate, compliant, and ready to send.
* **Trigger:** Draft Listing Package is complete.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Draft package, QC checklist, platform rule profile, original intake/assets.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Run the QC checklist against every required deliverable.
  2. Check title, description, pricing, category/platform recommendation, photo order, posting instructions, and buyer replies.
  3. Confirm there are no policy or fit problems.
  4. Mark each category pass/fail.
  5. If any fail appears, return the draft for correction.
  6. Approve only when every required item passes.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Complete
  * Clear
  * Accurate
  * Compliant
  * Effective
  * Defect vs revision distinction applied correctly
* **Outputs:** QC-approved package or returned-for-rework package.
* **Done Criteria:** All required sections pass QC with no objective failures.
* **Next Stage:** Delivery & Posting Handoff
* **Common Exceptions:** Missing brand/specs, title violations, unrealistic pricing, wrong category, poor photo order logic, policy-risk wording.
* **Saved Records:** QC checklist result, pass/fail notes, defect codes, corrected draft version, approval timestamp.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** Usually none unless QC reveals a customer-supplied fact issue that needs clarification.
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: any checklist failure is a fail; defects are fixed free, while preference-based revisions are handled separately. **DECISION NEEDED:** final numeric QC thresholds still need founder approval.

## 10. SOP 7

* **SOP Name:** Delivery & Posting Handoff
* **Stage Purpose:** Deliver the approved package in a format the customer can use immediately and hand off posting cleanly.
* **Trigger:** QC passes.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Approved Listing Package, posting checklist, posting instructions, delivery message template.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Deliver the package in the approved format.
  2. Include title, description, price plan, photo order, category guidance, buyer replies, and posting checklist.
  3. Tell the customer exactly what to do next.
  4. Request the live URL or screenshots immediately after posting.
  5. Offer Posting Assist only if included or purchased.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Is the package copy/paste-ready?
  * Are instructions short enough to follow on mobile?
  * Is the “send URL after posting” instruction explicit?
  * Is optional support clearly bounded?
* **Outputs:** Delivered Listing Package and posting handoff instructions.
* **Done Criteria:** Customer has the final package and knows the next required action.
* **Next Stage:** Live Posting Verification
* **Common Exceptions:** Customer confusion, customer asks UtahLister to post for them, unsupported platform request, request for extra revisions after delivery.
* **Saved Records:** Delivered file version, delivery timestamp, delivery message, included checklist version.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “Your listing package is ready. Post it from your account, then send us the live URL or screenshots.”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: customer posts from their own account; UtahLister may guide but does not normalize account access. Recommended assumption: Posting Assist is optional, bounded, and separate unless included in the lane.

## 11. SOP 8

* **SOP Name:** Live Posting Verification
* **Stage Purpose:** Confirm the listing is actually live, matches the package, and is usable for tracking.
* **Trigger:** Customer says the item is posted or sends URL/screenshots.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Live URL preferred, screenshots fallback, delivered package, posting checklist.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Open the live listing or review screenshots.
  2. Check title, price, photos, and obvious category fit against the delivered package.
  3. Confirm the listing is visible/live.
  4. If something is wrong, send a fix checklist and re-verify.
  5. If no URL is available, accept screenshots plus listing title and timestamp as fallback evidence.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Is the listing live?
  * Does it materially match the package?
  * Is there enough proof to continue into tracking?
* **Outputs:** Live verification status, correction request if needed, verified listing evidence.
* **Done Criteria:** Listing is verified live or routed into an exception path.
* **Next Stage:** URL Logging & 48-Hour Review
* **Common Exceptions:** Incorrect posting, listing not visible, no URL provided, customer delay.
* **Saved Records:** URL, screenshots, verification timestamp, correction notes, re-verification status.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “We checked your listing. It’s live / needs these fixes before we can verify it.”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: Stage 9 outputs are non-negotiable — URL preferred, screenshot fallback acceptable.

## 12. SOP 9

* **SOP Name:** URL Logging & 48-Hour Review
* **Stage Purpose:** Capture the live listing record, review early performance, and recommend one decisive optimization if needed.
* **Trigger:** Listing is verified live.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Verified URL/screenshots, posting timestamp, item details, pricing plan, tracking template.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Log the listing URL and baseline data.
  2. Start the 48-hour timer.
  3. At 48 hours, review views, saves, inquiries, visible issues, and customer-reported feedback.
  4. Capture screenshots and note metrics.
  5. If weak performance appears, recommend one primary change rather than a scattershot rewrite.
  6. If the listing sold, capture the result and move toward proof closeout.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Was the URL logged immediately?
  * Were screenshots captured as visual proof?
  * Were customer edits documented if they changed the live listing?
  * Was the optimization recommendation specific and trackable?
* **Outputs:** URL log entry, 48-hour review note, optimization recommendation, updated metrics record.
* **Done Criteria:** Review completed and logged; included optimization advice delivered if needed.
* **Next Stage:** Proof Capture & Closeout
* **Common Exceptions:** Missing URL, late posting, incorrect posting, weak early performance, customer edits distort measurement.
* **Saved Records:** URL log, metrics at post and 48h, screenshots, issues found, actions taken, follow-up outcome.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “Your listing has been up 48 hours. Here’s what we’re seeing and the one change we recommend.”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: UtahLister logs URLs, runs a 48-hour review, and uses proof carefully. Recommended assumption: one-week follow-up remains optional unless added to the service lane.

## 13. SOP 10

* **SOP Name:** Proof Capture & Closeout
* **Stage Purpose:** Build the evidence packet, mark the job complete, and preserve learnings for proof and dispute defense.
* **Trigger:** 48-hour review is complete, or customer declines further optimization.
* **Owner:** UtahLister Founder/Operator
* **Required Inputs:** Intake record, payment record, delivered package, verified URL/screenshots, 48-hour review notes, customer messages, sale/proof artifacts if available.
* **Step-by-Step Actions:**

  1. Assemble the evidence packet in chronological order.
  2. Save the final URLs, screenshots, and review notes.
  3. Save any sale outcome, testimonial, or before/after proof if available.
  4. Mark job as Complete, Completed – Proof Missing, or Closed under exception policy.
  5. Send final completion message.
  6. Log lessons learned and any reusable proof.
* **Internal Checks:**

  * Does the packet include intake, payment, deliverable, posting evidence, and review evidence?
  * Are timestamps clear?
  * Are proof artifacts reusable for case studies or dispute defense?
  * Was the final status applied correctly?
* **Outputs:** Evidence packet, closed job, reusable proof record, lessons-learned note.
* **Done Criteria:** Evidence packet stored and job status finalized.
* **Next Stage:** None — workflow complete
* **Common Exceptions:** Proof missing, customer dissatisfaction, refund request, no sale proof available, incomplete handoff evidence.
* **Saved Records:** Chronological timeline, key customer messages, payment references, final URL/screenshots, completion acknowledgment, testimonial if any, case-study fields if relevant.
* **Customer-Facing Communication:** “Your job is complete. We’ve closed the file and saved the final record.”
* **Notes:**
  Confirmed workflow rule: proof capture is not just a screenshot; it is a stored evidence packet.

## 14. Cross-Stage Rules That Apply Everywhere

* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Customer posts from their own account. UtahLister does not normalize password collection or account takeover. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Seller keeps the item, buyer handoff, and payout. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Payment clears before production starts. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** One item equals one Listing Pack unless a bundle is explicitly defined. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** UtahLister does not guarantee sale, price, or timeline. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Risky or restricted items are paused or rejected early.  
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Incomplete intake pauses the clock. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Every job needs saved proof of intake, payment, delivery, posting evidence, and closeout.  
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Defects and revisions are not the same; defects are fixed under QC, revisions are bounded by scope.  
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** Keep customer-facing statuses simple even if internal workflow is more detailed. 
* **Confirmed workflow rule:** No raw passwords, IDs, exact home addresses, or unrelated personal files in the working database. Treat imported files/screenshots as untrusted input.  
* **Recommended assumption:** Founder/Operator owns every stage until delegation exists; do not design this SOP set around a team that does not exist yet.
